UI/UX Design for iPhone app

Client: Case Study

Role & Responsibilities: UI/UX Designer

Deliverables: Personas, User Research, Storyboards, sitemap, wireframe and prototype, visual design

Tools & Software Used: Axure, Photoshop, Visio

Summary: Designed a prototype of an iPhone and iPad app to help visitors navigate in Yellowstone National Park, plan their trip, and share photos and itinerary on social media or by email

User Research

7 potential users were interviewed regarding what information they look for when traveling to a national park

Questions:

  1. When you travel, do you want to share your experience with friends through photos and posts on social media?
  2. What apps do you like to use and why?
  3. What features of those apps would you like improved or what frustrates you with these apps?
  4. Which is more important to you when traveling – recording and sharing your experience with friends and family back home or finding out information during your trip on local attractions, restaurants, etc.? or are they equally important?
  5. What are the top 3 things that are important to you when visiting national parks? i.e. spotting animals, lodging, visitor centers, lookout points, gas, food, restrooms, hiking trails, etc.

User Interview: Is information on the park or ability to share your experience such as photos more important or equally important?

User Persona

Persona – Andy

  • Age 30
  • Lives in Los Angeles
  • Travels by himself or with others
  • Likes traveling internationally and to national parks
  • Likes hiking, biking, camping
  • Likes taking pictures of scenery, animals, of himself doing outdoor activities and traveling
  • Wants to share travel experience with friends and family
  • Has iPhone, iPad
  • Uses Instagram, Twitter, Facebook

Job story

When I travel, I want to record the experience through photos and notes and find nearby attractions, so I can spend more time doing instead of planning and share my experience with friends and family and for personal review.

User frustrations

  • Dislikes being disconnected from internet, wi-fi
  • Doesn’t want to miss opportunity to see or do something when in the area
  • Doesn’t like to take a lot of time doing research

User successes

  • Likes getting information quickly
  • Likes being able to share his experience such as photos with friends and family

Prototype

1. User selects place to visit: Mammoth Hot Springs

2. User views visitor center hours and information on what to see at Mammoth Hot Springs

3. User can add Mammoth Hot Springs to My Trip, which creates an itinerary

4. User can add own photo to personal album or share to Gallery, social media or email

5. User can view Gallery of photos uploaded by other users

6. User can view map showing sites, hiking trails, and locations where animals have been seen
