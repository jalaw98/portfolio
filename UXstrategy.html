UX Strategy

Description: Completed apprenticeship with Jaime Levy, author of UX Strategy, to design a minimal viable product - a mobile-responsive app - to help brides-to-be find wedding reception venues.

Process: Conducted user interviews, created persona, conducted competitive research, created landing page to gage consumer interest, created wireframes and prototype, and conducted user testing on prototype to improve design.

Deliverables:

  • Persona
  • Competitive Research
  • Storyboard
  • Landing Page
  • Wireframes
  • Design of mobile-responsive app

Customer Problem

Brides-to-be who plan to get married in a church or synagogue in Los Angeles have difficulty finding wedding venues that are affordable near the ceremony site.

Persona

Amanda

Description:

  • mid 30s
  • Has full-time job in advertising
  • Engaged and planning wedding in 6 months

Behavior:

  • Uses Yelp to see reviews on restaurants and services before going
  • Uses Airbnb to book places to stay
  • Concerned about her guests from out-of-town – wants the reception site to be convenient to her ceremony site

Needs & Goals:

  • Wants to find a wedding venue near her ceremony site (a church)
  • Wants to be able to book wedding venue without needing to do a lot of research to find other services for the venue i.e. catering, DJ, limo service, florist
  • Needs to find wedding venue within her budget
  • Doesn’t want to spend a lot of time researching all of the other services needed for the wedding – wants to find everything associated with the wedding venue in one place

Competitive Research

The Knot

Description

  • All-inclusive wedding planning resource

Strengths

  • Can organize, manage budget and track payments with budget tool
  • Get inspired and see ideas for dress, hairstyles, makeup, cakes, décor, vendors, and more.

Weaknesses

  • No comparison feature
  • Not mobile-friendly
  • No catering menu samples
  • No actual prices listed

Perfect Wedding Venue

Description

  • Provides real estate agents for wedding venues

Strengths

  • Good filtering system, large selection of venues in Los Angeles

Weaknesses

  • Not mobile-friendly
  • No comparison feature – only add

Wedding Wire

Description

  • Online marketplace connecting merchants with engaged couples and party planners

Strengths

  • Good comparison feature

  • No filter by price
